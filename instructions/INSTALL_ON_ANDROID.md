# install on android

To install RIME on an android phone / tablet, here is one way you can do it:

1: Go to the F-droid website: https://f-droid.org/en/
and download F-droid

<img src="../images\android\a001gotofdroidorg.jpg" width="600" height="400">

2: In the download folder, click install

<img src="../images\android\a002_indownloadfolderclick.jpg" width="600" height="400">

3: You might receive a warning saying that your phone or tablet wont allow 
installing apps from this unknown source. You then have to change the settings to allow it. 
First, click the settings button:

<img src="../images\android\a003_warning.jpg" width="600" height="400">

4: Click on the togglebutton on the right:

<img src="../images\android\a004_togglesetting.jpg" width="600" height="400">

5: You can now install F-droid:

<img src="../images\android\a005_clickinstall.jpg" width="600" height="400">

6: When the installation is done, you should see the F-droid icon:

<img src="../images\android\a006_fdroidicon.jpg" width="600" height="400">

7: When you click on the icon, you should see a loading screen like this. 
You then have to wait until the loading is finished: 

<img src="../images\android\a007_fdroidloading.jpg" width="600" height="400">

8: When finished, you should see a search button in the lower right:

<img src="../images\android\a008_searchicon.jpg" width="600" height="400">

9: CLick on it to search for "trime":

<img src="../images\android\a009_lookforrime.jpg" width="600" height="400">

10: When you have found it, click download and install

<img src="../images\android\a010_downloadrime.jpg" width="600" height="400">

11: When installed, you should see the Trime icon:

<img src="../images\android\a011_serimeicon.jpg" width="600" height="400">

12: Connect the phone/tablet to a pc using a usb cable.
You should then see a window like this where you can access the files on 
the phone/tablet from the pc:

<img src="../images\android\a012_connecttablettopc.jpg" width="600" height="400">

13: Go to the rime folder and paste the Liuma .yaml files into the folder:

<img src="../images\android\a013_pasteyamlfiles.jpg" width="600" height="400">

14: Open the default.cusom.yaml file and verify that that the Liumafan and Liumajian 
schemas are in the file:

<img src="../images\android\a014_checkschemasadded.jpg" width="600" height="400">

15: Disconnect the phone/tablet from the pc. Now you might have to restart the phone/tablet
to make the Rime app aware of the files you added.
After restart, click the Rime icon on the phone / tablet. You should then see the Rime
app load the data:

<img src="../images\android\a015_rimeloading.jpg" width="600" height="400">

16: Once the app has stopped loading, click on the Schemata text on the top left of the app.
You should then see a radio button where you can shoose between the two
schemas you added: Liumafan and Liumajian. Then select the one you want:

<img src="../images\android\a016_selectliumaversion.jpg" width="600" height="400">

17: Leave / close the app and open an app where you can write text, eg. a browser.
You should then see the Liuma keyboard and be able to write. In this case i wrote "uod"
which for example writes the character 份 or the word 你好.
You can switch to english by clicking the 中文 button in the lower left.

<img src="../images\android\a017_useliuma.jpg" width="600" height="400">

end.
